This is a sample polls application that demonstrates the use of Django web framework.

Problem

When companies come to campus, they often hold talks or events and the students usually need to login, write names, or sign-in so they can have the students information There is also a difficulty to measure the impact of events

Proposed Solution

Implement a responsive web application for logging in at the beginning of events Have a mobile application on devices and students choose to login and logout at the end of events Events will then be posted on the web or mobile application and students will take an impact survey after each event Once the student completes the survey they gain points which they can accumulate throughout the semester The points can be used in a raffle draw at the end of the semester to provide the winner with meaningful prizes/swag

Effect

Attendance at events will increase Student's reactions/feedback will be tracked throughout the semester Students will gain new information attending these events
